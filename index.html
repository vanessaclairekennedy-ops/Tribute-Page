A man of many identities:

Let's Learn About Dan

He has many identities.

  • Teacher
  • Writer
  • Musician
  • Dad
  • Best Friend

Let's start with...

Teacher

Dan started teaching a little later in life. And I can't help but think that has made him a better teacher. He treats his students with respect. Instills empathy and weaves multiple perspectives into his lessons. Without fail, he always has at least one student each year confess that his class kept them going. Very few teachers can say the same.

He is also a...

Writer

When I first learned Dan was a writer, I was instantly interested in his work. I read anything I could get my grubby mits on. It felt like a secret window into his past, Dan before he became a teacher. And after seven years together, he has started writing again. I can't wait to read what he creates next (or I could never see it and that is OK too!)

If you would like to know more, please refer to his bio on Amazon.

Don't forget he is a...

Musician

I haven't gotten to hear as much as I would like of his music. It took me a bit of digging to even find the video above. But whenever I see him perform, he does so with a level of confidence I can never muster. His lyrics are intellegent. His voice is one of my most favorite sounds, and I often replay the videos he recorded early on in our relationship.

Two people call him...

Dad

He really is the best dad for our kids. Incredibly patient with Eliot when she is stimming out or emotionally disregulated. He is able to laugh when Rilo is acting like a complete animal but cooly redirects him when it gets messy. I do not know how many people could calmly exist around 2 screeching children as they stampede through the house. Yeah, we both have our moments, but, for the most part, he swims through our chaos mostly unscathed. Somehow.

Only I get to call him...

Best Friend

Pretty much the best and only person for me. He is smart, honest, handsome, hilarious, fascinating, thoughtful, moody, gentle, incredibly organized, and consistant. He stayed through my unexpected changes--started out dating a blonde cis-het woman. He ended up with a brown-haired pansexual, genderfluid queer. And he still loves me. I am not sure how I got so lucky, but here I am. Gotta say, life is pretty good.
